How is Treaty 5 Being Implented Today

Treaty 5 Chiefs and Grand Chiefs unveil a plan of action on racism in the wake of the Manitoba government's policy perspectives on residential schools.

Educator Connie Wyatt Anderson will teach viewers about the intriguing history of Treaty 5, what made it unusual, and how it relates to OCN and The Pas. She will also discuss three photographs in the Sam Waller Museum's collection that relate to Treaty 5 in surprising ways.
